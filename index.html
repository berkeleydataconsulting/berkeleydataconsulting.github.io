Berkeley Data Consulting

We leverage a powerful combination of data science, economics, and engineering to deliver scalable data-driven insights that power business decisions. Our expertise in analytics, modeling, and infrastructure development allows us to architect innovative solutions that help our clients harness the potential of data.

We originally came together to create UC Berkeley’s first course that applies data science techniques to economic concepts, through which we’ve now taught more than 100 students. While working closely to create this popular course, we realized that we share a passion for leveraging data science to solve key problems in various domains.

Now, we use our combined experience from industry to provide valuable solutions for organizations.
